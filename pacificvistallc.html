Our Process

Wholesale real estate, reinvented.

We acquire properties below market value, then assign the purchase contracts to end buyers, all without ever holding the asset on our books. No renovations, no landlord risk, no long hold periods.

Our AI platform supercharges every stage of this proven strategy, from lead generation to contract execution, compressing weeks of work into hours.

01

AI Driven Lead Identification

Our proprietary models scan thousands of off market signals daily, distressed sellers, pre foreclosures, absentee owners, and expired listings, to surface the highest probability opportunities before anyone else.

02

Rapid Valuation and Underwriting

Machine learning models trained on millions of comparable transactions produce accurate ARV estimates and offer prices in minutes, not days. We move decisively when others are still researching.

03

Seller Outreach and Contract

Our team uses AI assisted communication tools to engage motivated sellers empathetically and efficiently, securing contracts at favorable terms with speed and precision.

04

Buyer Matching and Assignment

A curated network of vetted cash buyers is matched to each deal algorithmically, ensuring fast closings and maximum assignment fees on every transaction.

Our Technology

Proprietary AI at every layer

We built our technology stack from the ground up to solve the exact friction points in wholesale real estate. The result is a competitive advantage that compounds with every deal we close.

⚙

Predictive Market Intelligence

Real time ingestion of MLS data, tax records, permit filings, and demographic trends feeds predictive models that flag neighborhoods before price movement occurs.

📈

Automated Comparative Analysis

Our ARV engine runs comps at machine speed with human level judgment, adjusting for property condition, micro location, and current buyer demand in any given zip code.

👥

Seller Motivation Scoring

NLP models analyze hundreds of behavioral and situational signals to rank leads by conversion probability, so our acquisitions team focuses only on sellers ready to move.

🔗

Buyer Network Matching

Each deal is cross referenced against buyer preferences, past purchase history, and current capital availability to find the fastest and highest value assignment path.

📄

Smart Contract Automation

AI assisted document generation and review cuts paperwork time from days to minutes, while flagging risk clauses before they become costly mistakes.

📊

Portfolio Performance Analytics

Transparent dashboards give partners real time visibility into deal pipeline, assignment margins, and capital velocity across every active transaction.
